About this Platform

Our platform is designed to empower teachers with the tools to easily monitor and track the health of children under the age of five. By measuring essential health metrics such as height, weight, body temperature, and heart rate every time a child attends class, the platform enables teachers to gain valuable insights into each child's growth and overall well-being. In addition to individual data, the platform offers comprehensive statistical visualizations for entire classrooms, allowing for easy comparison and trend analysis. This innovative solution also supports tracking key health indicators like body mass index (BMI) and respiratory rate, ensuring that potential concerns are identified early, fostering healthier learning environments for all children.
